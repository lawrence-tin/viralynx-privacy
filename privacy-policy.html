Privacy Policy for Viralynx

Last Updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

Viralynx ("we", "our", or "us") is a social media analytics tool that helps content creators and brands understand their performance across YouTube and Facebook platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your Facebook Page to Viralynx, we collect:

  • Page Information: Page name, Page ID, profile picture, and cover photo
  • Post Content: Text, images, videos, and links from your Page's public posts
  • Engagement Metrics: Likes, comments, shares, and reactions on your posts
  • Performance Data: Views, reach, impressions, and other analytics Facebook provides

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Display your social media analytics in our dashboard
  • Generate performance reports and insights
  • Train machine learning models to predict content performance
  • Improve our analytics algorithms

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Snowflake, a enterprise-grade cloud data warehouse. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption at rest and in transit. Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Aggregated, anonymized data may be used for research and product improvement.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about your Page
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (see Data Deletion Instructions below)
  • Disconnect your Facebook Page at any time

7. Data Retention

We retain your analytics data as long as your account is active. When you disconnect your Facebook Page or request deletion, we remove your data within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email or through the dashboard.
