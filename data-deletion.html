Data Deletion Instructions for Viralynx

This page provides instructions on how to request deletion of your Facebook Page data from Viralynx.

Option 1: Delete Data Directly Within Viralynx

Step 1: Log into your Viralynx account
Step 2: Go to Settings → Connected Accounts
Step 3: Find your Facebook Page connection
Step 4: Click "Disconnect" or "Delete Data"
Step 5: Confirm deletion when prompted

Option 2: Request Deletion by Email

Step 1: Send an email to privacy@viralynx.com
Step 2: Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request - Viralynx"
Step 3: Include in your email:
- Your Facebook Page Name and Page ID
- Your name and contact information
- A statement confirming you are authorized to make this request
Step 4: We will process your request within 7 business days

Option 3: Remove Via Facebook App Settings

Step 1: Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
Step 2: Find "Viralynx" in your list of connected apps
Step 3: Click "Remove"
Step 4: Check "Delete all posts, videos and events created by Viralynx" (if applicable)
Step 5: Click "Confirm"

What Happens When You Request Deletion

  • All Facebook Page data (posts, engagement metrics, analytics) will be permanently removed from our database
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered after 30 days
  • Aggregated, anonymized statistics (not traceable to your Page) may be retained for research
  • You will receive a confirmation email once deletion is complete

Need Help?

If you need assistance with data deletion, contact us:

Email: privacy@viralynx.com

Response Time: Within 48 hours
